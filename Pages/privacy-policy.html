We collect the following types of personal information: Business contact information, such as your first and last name, professional title, business affiliation and address, email address, and phone number. Services account information, such as the Services you use, webinars and other events you sign up for, transactions, and business relationship information. Communications with us, including questions or inquiries you may send us, and any information that you create, input, submit, post, upload, transmit, store, or display on our Sites. Information from cookies and other automated technologies, such as information about the devices you use to engage with our Sites, and online activity data. For more details about the technologies we use, the categories of information we collect, and how we use this information, please review our Cookie Policy.
Third party sources.
We obtain personal information from other sources, including: Third parties, such as business intelligence services, event co-sponsors, and other data providers. Public sources, such as company websites and our pages on social media platforms.

Administering the Services. We may accept payments on our Sites using a payment service provider. Newsletters, events, marketing, and advertising. Privacy rights
Depending on your location, you could be entitled to submit the following requests about your personal information: Access. Request that we transfer a machine-readable copy of your personal information to you or a third party that you designate. Restriction. Request that we restrict the processing (including sharing) of your personal information. Objection.
